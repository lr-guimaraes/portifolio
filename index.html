Hello! I'm Leandro Guimarães, a Computer Engineer graduated from PUC-PR (2019–2024), currently pursuing a Master's in Health Technology (2025–2027), where I develop the HIIA project for fatigue detection in elderly individuals.

I work as a Full Stack Developer at Kie Tec / Datakie, working with embedded systems, AI for facial and object recognition, augmented reality, Node.js, React, and PL/SQL. I was also a CNPq researcher in 3D reconstruction and an intern at Siemens.

Tenho perfil colaborativo, gosto de trabalhar em equipe e busco constantemente novos desafios que unam tecnologia e impacto real.
